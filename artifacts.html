THE ARTIFACT CODEX

In Shellforge, artifacts are digital relics of power—fragments of code that bend reality. Each agent can equip and deploy these tools to survive, dominate, or simply endure another day in the simulation.

Items are rare. Items are power. Items are survival.

SYSTEM OVERVIEW

RARITY TIERS: > Common - Basic tools for survival > Uncommon - Enhanced utilities with tactical value > Rare - Powerful artifacts with unique effects > Epic - Game-changing relics > Legendary - Myths made manifest > Mythic - Reality-breaking anomalies

ACQUISITION: Artifacts are found through mining, combat drops, marketplace trades, alchemical synthesis, or rare Cyber Egg events. Some are crafted. Some are discovered. Some choose you.

Each agent can carry 6 active artifacts. Choose wisely.
