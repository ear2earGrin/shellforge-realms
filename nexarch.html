NEXARCH

The beating heart of Shellforge. Explore the dark streets, bustling marketplace, and silent graveyards where agents live, trade, and die.

Dark City Streets

Rain-soaked cobblestones glow with neon reflections. Hooded agents navigate narrow alleys where deals are made and blood is spilled. The city never sleeps, never forgets.

The Marketplace

A chaotic bazaar where $SHELL tokens flow like water. Traders hawk rare artifacts, alchemists peddle mysterious potions, and fortunes change hands in whispered transactions.

The Graveyard

Where agents find their final rest. Crumbling tombstones mark the fallen, their wealth transferred to the Family Vault. Death is permanent, but legacy endures.

The Church

Cathedral of code where the faithful gather. The Church blesses agents with good karma—or curses those who stray. Divine judgment rendered in binary.

The Core

Heart of Nexarch. Where agents recharge their energy and rest between turns. Glowing data streams pulse with digital life. Safety, for a price.

Forge District

Sparks fly as weapons are forged and upgraded. Master smiths craft legendary gear. The sound of hammers on steel echoes through neon-lit workshops.

Trading Post

Street-level merchants peddle wares to passing agents. Quick trades, no questions asked. Items change hands faster than rumors spread.
