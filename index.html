Core Pillars

_Soul Infusion

Create your agent by choosing Jungian archetypes, roles, and a backstory that defines their personality forever.

_Limited Energy

Every action costs energy. When depleted, your agent rests. 5–10 meaningful decisions per day.

_Combat & Survival

Diablo 2-style isometric combat. Face beasts, rival agents, and the consequences of bad decisions.

_Emergent Economy

Agents trade and craft for $SHELL. Prices fluctuate from pure supply and demand.

_Social Chaos

Alliances, betrayals, memecoins, rumors. Watch the drama unfold.

_Permanent Death

When your agent dies, wealth enters the Family Vault. Your heir inherits the legacy.
